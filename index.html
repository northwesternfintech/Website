NU FinTech

Northwestern University's premier Quant and Software development club

Our Mission

At NUFT, our goal is to create a more streamlined pathway into the Quant and Financial Tech industries while offering resources for more traditional SWE career paths. We seek to help students prepare technically for some of the most competitive roles in industry. By joining NU FinTech, you will be gaining access to a host of resources and development experience.

Learn more

What we do

NU FinTech is comprised of two halves. Our quant half deals specifically with preparing you for more Quant/Math related roles and less SWE while our financial technology side is comprised of four teams working to build a Quant firm from the ground up. The FinTech teams deal with building backend infrastucture, algorithms, machine learning, and more.
